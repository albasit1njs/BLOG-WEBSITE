Do you know about Synthesia.io ?

Synthesia is a synthetic media generation company that develops software used to create AI generated video content. It is based in London, England.

How Synthesia.io Works:

Synthesia.io leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze and interpret text scripts. This process enables the platform to generate lifelike videos featuring AI-generated avatars, providing a unique and customizable visual representation of the content.
